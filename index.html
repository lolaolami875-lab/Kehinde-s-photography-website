About the Artist

Adebayo Kehinde is a 25-year-old Kwara-born, Ibadan-based visual storyteller and filmmaker. Photography has been his primary medium, capturing the richness of human experience. Gradually, he transitioned into mixed media (acrylic on canvas prints) and cinematography to enhance his storytelling.

His work explores the concept of "HOME", examining cultural and societal perspectives. Kehinde delves into the lives of men across various fields and explores familial relationships, with a particular focus on Yoruba culture.

His photography balances conceptual and documentary styles, capturing both imaginative and realistic aspects of his subjects. Kehinde has exhibited his works at Emerging Expression curated by Creanth and the Ibadan Art Fair.
